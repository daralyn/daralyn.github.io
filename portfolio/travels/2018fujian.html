Travel Day(s)

July 16-17, 2018

Today, we arrived at HNL around 8:00am, and were dropped off by my dad at the China Eastern Airlines check-in area. We got through security, and headed to the gate, which was a super long walk.
Our flight was delayed about an hour, and once we boarded we left the gate pretty quickly, but had to wait in traffic control line for another hour. While waiting, my friend and I were looking at the safety card instructions, trying to read it all in Chinese. The flight was about 10 hours, and not too smooth (for me at least).
Since our layover in Shanghai was originally 2 hours, but we were delayed about 2 hours in Honolulu, we had about 30 mins as soon as we landed to get to our next flight, to Fuzhou.
Waiting for us on the ground as we exited our first plane were some crews to help those of us on the Hnolulu to Shanghai flight to connect to our flight to Fuzhou. We we ran from where we were, got through immigration, and through securityin time to get to our gate, which we then had 2 mins to spare because we had to be shuttled to the plane.
The flight to Fuzhou was about 2 hours. Once we landed, we found the checked bags, and headed outside to get picked up by the hotel airport van.
We arrived at the hotel, checked in, and then walked around a little bit. We found a nice boardwalk area just a short walk away from the entrace to the hotel.
We then ate dinner at the hotel around 8:00pm, and had a Fuzhou special: fish balls. It was delicious, and had some meat filling on the inside.
