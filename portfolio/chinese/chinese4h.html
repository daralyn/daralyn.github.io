Chinese 4 Honors

Description

Below are videos that I made when I took Chinese 4 Honors in my sophomore year.

SECR Project

SECR stands for Spiritual, Ethical and Community Requirement. The Chinese 4H course included this credit. So we had to do a SECR project. I chose to tutor another student in Chinese. The video is about that service, and what I learned from it.

Morality Project

This video is another aspect of the SECR responsibilities. In this project, a classmate and I did research on sea turtles, and how they affect the environment. We also touched on the ethical viewpoint about poaching in China.

以前vs现在

In our Chinese class, we used the Integrated Chinese Level 2 part 2 book. One of the lessons includes Before vs Now for some cities. We did a project comparing a before and now view, by asking our parents or grandparents about their life. We then compared it to own lives today.

传统节日菜 Traditional Festival Foods

This video shows how to make dumplings, and also different ways to wrap them.
